Flag Race

Flag race is a jump quest where you clear 3 laps within the 8 minute time limit to get points for your guild for the week. The amount of points you get is determined by how quickly you can finish all 3 laps. Information regarding the flag race skills and scoring can be found here.

The jump quest cycles each weekly reset (Monday 12:00 AM UTC) between 3 maps:

  1. Daylight Snowfield
  2. Sunset Snowfield
  3. Night Snowfield
caution

These points can be acquired by either killing 1,000 monsters or weekly bosses until you have accrued 40 boss points.

Entries for Flag Race are disabled on Sunday 11:00 PM UTC to Monday 1:00 AM UTC.
